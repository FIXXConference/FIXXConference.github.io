A one-day discussion on how to improve the tech industry.

Spring, 2016 • Cleveland, Ohio

About the Conference

FIXX is a conference supporting diversity in technology fields, with an emphasis on design and software/web development. We aim to draw attention to necessary changes in company culture in order to both attract and retain qualified and ambitious talent. Held annually in April, FIXX is a one-day, one-track event, with speakers in the morning and breakout discussion groups in the afternoon. We believe this format will optimize the ability to learn from each other’s experiences on what works and doesn’t work in improving work culture. We aim to strengthen the technology industry in Ohio for both men and women and work towards a diverse, inclusive community for anyone. Want to join the conversation? Come to FIXX!
